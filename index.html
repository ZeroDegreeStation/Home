Access & Location

How to Reach Snow Station

We are conveniently located just 800 meters from Meiho Ski Resort main entrance. Multiple transportation options available.

By Car

From Tokyo: Take Highway 17 to Nagano exit. Follow signs to Meiho Ski Resort. Free parking available.

Travel time: Approximately 3 hours from Tokyo

By Train

Take Shinkansen to Nagano Station, then local train to Meiho Station. We provide pickup service from station (advance reservation required).

Travel time: 2.5 hours from Tokyo Station

By Bus

Direct overnight buses available from Shinjuku to Meiho Ski Resort. Stop is 5-minute walk from our guest house.

Travel time: 4 hours from Shinjuku
